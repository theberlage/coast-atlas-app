# Battery Vineta  

The battery Vineta played a crucial role in defending the entrance to the Nieuwe Waterweg and Rotterdam Harbor. The temporality of its function changed, originally a Dutch gun battery, it was repurposed by the Germans in 1943, and renamed Battery Vineta. The site included artillery bunkers, battle shelters, work station, munition storage, sleeping bunkers, tobroek, commander post.
